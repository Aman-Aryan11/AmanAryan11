VaidyAI Project

VaidyAI aims to centralize, categorize and simplify healthcare record management. By streamlining access to patient histories for doctors and personal medical records for individuals, it ensures informed decisions and empowers users. It is designed for seamless data categorization and secure sharing, VaidyAI reduces redundancy while prioritizing privacy. It bridges the gap between patients and providers, enhancing care and efficiency.

DrugHIVE Project

DrugHIVE is focused on structure-based drug design using a deep hierarchical generative model. It leverages the power of machine learning to overcome the limitations of traditional virtual screening methods in drug discovery. This deep hierarchical variational autoencoder outperforms state-of-the-art methods in speed, scalability, and performance, enabling efficient molecular generation and optimization.
